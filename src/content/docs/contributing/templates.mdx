Use these templates when creating new pages. Copy the relevant template, fill in the sections, and remove placeholder text.

## Setup guide template

````mdx
---
title: "[Name] Setup"
description: "Install and configure [thing]."
---

## Goal

What this guide helps you achieve.

## Prerequisites

- [Prerequisite 1](/link/)
- [Prerequisite 2](/link/)

## Tested on

> **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
> **Hardware:** [hardware details]
> **Software:** [OS, Python version, LeRobot version, etc.]

## Steps

### 1. First step

Explanation and command.

### 2. Second step

Explanation and command.

## Verify it works

How to confirm success. Include a command or expected output.

## Common issues

| Problem | Likely cause | Fix |
|---|---|---|
| [symptom] | [cause] | [fix] |

## Next step

Link to the next page in the workflow.
````
